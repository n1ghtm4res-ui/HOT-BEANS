JOB SPECIFICATION

Web Analyst

Web analysts collect and analyze data about website traffic and user behavior. They use this data to improve website performance and user experience.​

UI/UX Designer​

UI/UX designers create the user interface and user experience of a website. They make sure that the website is easy to use and visually appealing​

Quality Assurance Tester

Quality assurance testers test websites and other digital products to find and fix bugs. They make sure that products are working correctly and meet the needs of users.​

Web Developer

Web developers design, build, and maintain websites. They are responsible for the website's look, feel, and functionality.​

Digital Marketing Specialist​

Digital marketing specialists help companies promote their products and services online. They use a variety of tools and techniques to reach potential customers.​

Back-End Developer

Back-end developers focus on the server-side of a website. They create the logic that powers the website
